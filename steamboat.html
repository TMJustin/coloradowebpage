Steamboat Springs

City Overview

Colorado would be imcomplete without mention of its incredible mountain towns and resorts. Steamboat Springs is one these destinations and is hugely popular for residents and tourists alike. The small population of 13,244 is pretty indicative of the size of many mountain towns, but, Steamboat also sees thousands and thousands of tourists every year who come to partake in the recreation and relaxation that Steamboat Springs offers. While many resort towns are known for the busy season during the winter in which many people come to ski and snowboard, Steamboat also sees as lot of tourism in the Summer when those same slopes are used for hiking and mountain biking. Relaxation and recreation await anyone who finds themselves in Steamboat Springs. The city has been a staple destination for Coloradoans and out of state tourists since it was founded in 1900.

Source: United States Census Bureau

Region and Economy

Steamboat is located a few hours from Denver in the north-western area of the state in the Yampa Valley. Luckily the scenic drive through the Rockies is worth the three hour trip from Denver. Many of Colorado's mountain towns aren't that populated in terms of long time residents but as tourism sites they bring in a lot of money and primarily have economies that are based on that tourism. The average income that is enjoyed in Steamboat for year round residents is about $93,280.

Tourism and Activities

Steamboat Springs is major hub for tourism as most come to the mountain town during the Winter. One of Colorados premier ski resorts is here and many thousands of people come to the city every year to enjoy Winter sports. Luckily the slopes are very useful in other seasons as well and many will also come in warmer times to enjoy mountain biking and hiking or climbing. But the city of Steamboat has other offerings besides the surrounding mountains. Steamboat is a scenic place to take part in many of their local music and art festivals. Many of these annual activities bring people back year after year. For those who are looking for ultimate relaxation, strawberry hotsprings is just a quick drive up the mountain from Steamboat. These natural hotsprings are on the most beautiful places to visit. No trip would be complete without going to the hotsprings.
